Common Issues with Whirlpool Dryers

Whirlpool dryers are reliable appliances, but like all machines, they can experience issues over time. If you're facing problems with your Whirlpool dryer, our expert repair technicians can help diagnose and fix the issue quickly and efficiently. We offer Whirlpool dryer services in Vancouver, Burnaby, Coquitlam, and Surrey.

Issue Description Possible Causes Repair Solution
Dryer not heating The dryer runs, but clothes remain wet. Faulty heating element, broken thermostat, or clogged vents. Replace the heating element, thermostat, or clean the vents.
Excessive noise The dryer makes loud noises during operation. Worn-out drum rollers, damaged belt, or clogged exhaust. Replace drum rollers, belt, or clean the exhaust system.
Drum not spinning The drum doesn't rotate, and the clothes aren't tumbling. Broken belt or motor issues. Replace the belt or motor, depending on the cause.
Dryer won't turn on The dryer doesn't start at all. Faulty door switch, power supply issues, or a tripped breaker. Replace the door switch, check the power supply, or reset the breaker.

Common issues with Whirlpool dryers include heating problems, excessive noise, a non-spinning drum, or the dryer not turning on. These problems can usually be fixed by replacing faulty parts like the heating element, drum rollers, or motor.
