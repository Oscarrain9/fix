Eliminating mold odor in Samsung washing machine

The smell of mold in the washing machine may arise due to accumulation of moisture in the seals and pipes. To get rid of this unpleasant smell, try the following methods:

Start the cycle of washing at high temperature with added vinegar or special cleaning agents to remove mold.
After each wash cycle, leave the door open to dry the machine’s interior parts.
Clean the cleaning media and filter area regularly for dirt and debris.
These simple steps will help you get rid of the smell of mildew and keep fresh in your Samsung washing machine.

Leaks and leaks at the Samsung washing machine in Burnaby

If your Samsung washing machine leaks from the bottom or from the door, you should take steps to prevent damage and water leakage in your home. Some tips for eliminating leaks:

Check the door seals and replace them if necessary.
Check the water supply and drain hoses for cracks or leaks.
Inspect the body parts for damage and wear.
Regular checking and servicing of your Samsung washing machine will help avoid leaks and leaks, as well as extend the life of the device.
